A Canadian developed solution to nurse scheduling issues found uniquely amongst are health care system. Designed to:

  1. Increased Productivity: Save tremendous time for nurse managers / administration and nurses themselves through quick, seamless and real time scheduling changes.
  2. Increased Transparency: Allows everyone to equitably view the schedule and any changes with electronic documentation to ensure no misconceptions.
  3. Increased Team Cohesiveness/Fulfillment: Working to see which gaps to fill and helping each other out to successfully fill the schedule. Increases a sense of 'ownership' over one's schedule.
  4. Increased Organizational Efficiency: Metrics gleaned from software used to predict peak times for scheduling based on absences and other contingencies.
